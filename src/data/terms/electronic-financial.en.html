Chapter 2 Electronic Payment Gateway Services

Article 16 【Definitions】

① The definition of a term used in this chapter is as follows:

1. The term "electronic payment gateway service" refers to a service that transmits or receives payment information electronically in the purchase of goods or services, or that intermediates or processes the settlement of such payments.

Article 17 【Types of Electronic Payment Gateway Services】

① Electronic payment gateway service

1. Credit card payment service: When the user selects a credit card as the payment method, the Company transmits and receives credit card payment information through its electronic payment system and intermediates or processes the settlement of the payment.

2. Virtual account service: This refers to the settlement of the payment amount through the Company's electronic payment system by automatically issuing a unique one-time account for the user when the user wishes to pay it in cash.

3. Simple payment service: When the user selects a payment method provided by a simple payment service provider (e.g., electronic prepayment methods), the Company transmits and receives payment information through its electronic payment system and intermediates or processes the settlement of the payment.

② Electronic fund transfer gateway service: This service refers to the transmission and receipt of payment transaction information via the Company's electronic payment system and the delivery of virtual account information to facilitate or intermediate the settlement of funds when the user makes or receives a payment.

Article 18 【Limits】

The monthly cumulative payment amount and payment limit for each payment method may be restricted in accordance with the Company's service-specific policies and the policies of the relevant payment providers.

The definitions of terms used in this chapter are as follows:

1. The term "electronic prepayment means" refers to a prepaid electronic means granted to the user account issued by the Company under the Electronic Financial Transactions Act. It can be categorized into "VPay Pay Money," a paid prepaid means, and "VPay Points," a free prepaid means.

2. The term "VPay Pay Money" refers to a type of paid prepaid means that the user holds in their accounts either by purchasing it directly from the Company or by receiving it via transfers from other users. Users have full ownership and control over these funds, which may be used for simple transfers or payment transactions.

3. The term "VPay Points" refers to a type of unpaid prepaid means other than VPay Pay Money. These points are provided free of charge through the user's participation in services offered by the Company or designated third parties (collectively referred to as "Services, etc.").

4. The term "merchant" refers to the following persons:

a. An entity that, under contract with the Company, provides goods or services to users in exchange for electronic prepayment means;

b. An entity who acts as an intermediary in transactions involving prepaid instruments on behalf of another party that provides goods or services to users, in accordance with the Enforcement Decree of the Electronic Financial Transactions Act.

5. The term "simple transfer" refers to the transfer of a certain amount of VPay Pay Money from the user's balance to a recipient (the "Recipient") using methods provided by the Company. The Recipient receives the transferred amount as cash via a pre-registered primary account (the "Primary Account") or another designated account, according to the Company's predetermined procedures.
